Biography

Jonathan Hughes is an Associate Professor of Economics and a Fellow in the Renewable and Sustainable Energy Institute (RASEI) at the University of Colorado at Boulder. He received his Ph.D. in Transportation Technology and Policy from the University of California at Davis in 2009. He has a background in mechanical engineering (BSE, Duke University) and materials science (MSE, Cornell University). His research interests include recreation on public lands, climate change policy, freight transportation, traffic congestion, electricity and renewable energy markets.
